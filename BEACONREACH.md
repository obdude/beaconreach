# The Zen of BeaconReach (Value-Based ABM Outreach)

1.  **Value First.** Every interaction must deliver a standalone asset or insight (actionable benefit).
2.  **Clarity over Cleverness.** Be simple, direct, honest.
3.  **Assets over Asks.** Don't ask for time. Give a resource.
4.  **Transparent by Design.** Track interactions, be explicit about intentions.
5.  **Schema over Chaos.** Relationships are data. Structure them (JSON/CSV) so they survive the tool du jour.
6.  **Ownership is Liberty.** Never build your core business logic inside a "Black Box" SaaS.
7.  **Data-driven.** Decisions guided by signals, not assumptions.
8.  **Ethical, Always.** Respect privacy, consent, and attention.
9.  **Context is King.** A generic message is spam. A contextual message is a service. ("from annoying pest to welcome guest")
10.  **Low Frequency, High Signal.** Small touches accumulate trust. One great message beats ten nudges.
11. **Repeatable.** Processes can be manual, semi-automated, or fully automated.
12. **Automate the Process, Not the Human.** Use code to gather data, use humans to verify empathy.
13. **Open Source Forever.** The best GTM stacks are composable, transparent, and free from rent-seeking.

---

## Core Principles

1. **Recipient-centric**: Focus on their problems, needs, and context.
2. **Incremental value**: Each touchpoint adds something meaningful.
3. **Sustainable cadence**: Respect attention, avoid overwhelm.
4. **Tool-agnostic**: Implementable manually or with automation.
5. **Measurable**: Track, analyze, improve continuously.
6. **Transparent evolution**: Open for community improvements.
